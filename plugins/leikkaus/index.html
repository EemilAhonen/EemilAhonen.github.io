Leikkaus

High-end clipper plug-in

Free Download

Revolutionize Your Sound

Leikkaus is a dynamic clipper effect plugin.

Leikkaus is completely open-source. Check out the source code on GitHub!

Key Features:
- Detailed controls for clipping
- Supports and scales up to 4k monitors
- Cross-platform compatibility: Supports Windows and macOS
- Universal binary support: Compatible with both Intel and Apple Silicon processors

Dynamic Distortion Control

Unleash distortion mastery with our plugin's Input and Ceiling Gauges, perfect for precise gain and clipping adjustments. The Compensation Toggle ensures a smooth transition by adding the opposite of input to the output. Dive into dynamic distortion control effortlessly – no technical expertise needed. Transform your sound with ease.

Finely Tune Your Sound

Elevate your audio artistry with our plugin's Output Gauge and Mix Gauge, providing precise control over output levels and the blend between input and processed signals. The innovative Delta Toggle allows you to instantly hear the difference between your original and processed sound. Sculpt your sonic masterpiece effortlessly – adjust levels, find the perfect mix, and explore the nuances with the touch of a button. No need for a degree in audio engineering – immerse yourself in creativity and refine your sound with ease.

Intuitive UI Visualization

Explore our plugin's intuitive UI, showcasing original signals in gray and clipped signals in bold red. Effortlessly navigate distortion with clear visuals, allowing you to fine-tune your sound with confidence. Transform your audio experience with precision and creativity at your fingertips.
